Wong Foon Sien (A peer reviewed article from TVDSB Database)

This article talks about Wong Foon Sien who was a leading figure in the fight for human and civil rights for Chinese Canadians. In the 20th century Chinese Candians were considered residents of the British Empire but not Canadian citizens therefore they weren't granted full rights and privileges. Wong Foon Sien tirelessly worked for Chinese Canadian rights and laid a strong foundation for the future advancement of immigrants' rights in Canada.

Finding Employment

This articles tackles the issues Canadian immigrants have when finding jobs in their fields. According to studies from both Ontario and Alberta only 10-16% of immigrants had employment appropriate to their education/skill level.
